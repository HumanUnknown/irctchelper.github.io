# Privacy Policy for IRCTC Tatkal Helper

**Last Updated:** July 2026

## 1. Information We Collect
The **IRCTC Tatkal Helper** extension allows you to save passenger details to speed up the train ticket booking process. To function, the extension requires you to input:
- Passenger details (Name, Age, Gender)
- Travel preferences (Berth preference, Food preference, Coach preference)
- Contact information (Mobile Number)
- Identification and Billing details (GSTIN)

## 2. How Your Information is Stored
**All data is strictly stored locally on your device.** 
We use your browser's local storage API (`chrome.storage.local` / `browser.storage.local`) to save your passenger profiles. 

## 3. Data Transmission and Sharing
**We do not collect, transmit, or store your data on any external servers.** 
- No data is sent to us or any third-party servers. 
- The data only leaves the extension when you explicitly click the "Autofill Form" button, at which point the data is strictly inserted directly into the official IRCTC website's booking form that you are currently viewing.
- We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties.
- We do not use any telemetry, tracking tools, or analytics software.

## 4. Remote Code
This extension does not fetch or execute any remote code. All operational code is fully contained and bundled within the extension package itself.

## 5. Your Rights and Control
You have full control over your data. You can edit, clear, or delete any saved passenger profile at any time directly from the extension's user interface. Deleting a profile will instantly and permanently remove that data from your browser's local storage.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
